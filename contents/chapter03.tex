\chapter{Results}
 This chapter focuses on presenting the research data and analytical outcomes, supported by figures and tables, forming the empirical foundation of the dissertation.


\section{Content Elements}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Data Presentation: Display key data in a logical sequence, using figures (e.g., graphs, histograms) or tables.
    \item Figure and Table Captions: Ensure each figure or table has a clear title and caption, making it independently understandable.
    \item Results Description: Describe data in the order of figures/tables, emphasizing findings relevant to the research objectives.
    \item Major Achievements: Highlight the study’s key results and their alignment with the research problem.


\end{itemize}

Two references are inserted here~\cite{doi:10.1021/jp211439d,PhysRevB.83.075403}.


\section{Writing Tips}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Present data objectively, reserving interpretation for the Discussion chapter.
    \item Design figures and tables with clear labels and appropriate scales, ensuring consistency with the text.
    \item Organize data by research objectives or hypotheses to maintain logical flow.
    \item Briefly note data reliability and reproducibility (e.g., number of replicates or error margins).
\end{itemize}
